SMART CITY

It is famous for the Nagpur Orange and is known as the "Orange City" for being a major trade centre of oranges cultivated in the region. Nagpur is the biggest producer of Oranges in India with many large Orange gardens located in Nagpur and nearby Areas. Nagpur oranges are best in quality and rich in vitamin and nutrition.
