Why Nagpur Is Called Orange City ?

It is famous for the Nagpur Orange and is known as the "Orange City" for being a major trade centre of oranges cultivated in the region. For centuries, Nagpur has been famous for its orange gardens in the country, hence the name "Orange City".Over 70 % of the oranges in India come from Nagpur.Nagpur is the biggest producer of Oranges in India with many large Orange gardens located in Nagpur and nearby areas. Nagpur oranges are best in quality and rich in vitamin and nutrition.Maharashtra is the orange's largest producer state in India with approximately 8 lakh tonnes of production every year.Nagpur oranges exported across the globe every year.Also you will find very cheap rates of oranges in nagpur.

Nagpur's Airport
Nagpur's airport became the first airport in the country to receive an ISO 27000 certificate.

The Zero Mile marker at Nagpur is considered as India's geographical centre.
