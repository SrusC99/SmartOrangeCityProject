Do pubs in Nagpur serve orange juice?
I thought I’d start with a joke. Nagpur…famous for oranges…non-alcoholic beverage in a bar….get it?

Ok, I may be bad at telling jokes, but I am definitely good at telling you all about the top pubs in Nagpur. And, I am going to prove it with this list.


1. 10 Downing Street, Nagpur

Designed on the lines of a ‘proper English pub’, 10 Downing Street or TDS, as it is fondly called, is one of the oldest pubs in Nagpur. With a cozy ambiance given by mood lighting, softly lilting music in the background and smooth service, TDS is ‘the’ place to take your out-of-town friends when you want a great evening. Not one of the disco pubs in Nagpur, rather TDS is more a place for families and friends to catch up with no loud music or pushy crowds.
Location: Milestone Building, Wardha Road, Ramdaspeth, Nagpur
Timings: 12.30 pm – 3.30 pm and 7.30 pm – 11.30 pm
Must try: Cosmopolitan and the Chicken Platter
Cost for Two: ₹ 1000 (approx.)


2. Fionaa Lounge & Restaurant, Nagpur

A charming place with separate sections for the sit-down crowd and the dance-the-night-away jitterbugs, Fionaa offers a curious mix of a fine-dining restaurant and party lounge. An interesting drinks menu with delish cocktails, the DJ spinning groovy tunes and a fresh vibe make Fionaa a good choice if you’re looking for pubs in Nagpur with a dance floor. Plus, they also have live screening of sports events, giving you another reason to come here.
Location: Dharampeth Tower, Dharampeth
Timings: 12.00 noon – 11.30 pm
Must try: Cocktail, Veg Spring Rolls and Murgh Patiala
Cost for Two: ₹ 1000 (approx.)


3. Longitude, Nagpur

With its delicious and inventive cocktails and a wide array of spirits served in whimsical glasses, Longitude is one of the more refined nightclubs in Nagpur. Polished interiors in wood and a welcoming palette of colors, soft Jazz in the background and the friendliest staff you’ll encounter make a visit to Longitude a memorable one.
Location: Le Meridian, Wardha Road
Timings: 4.00pm – 1.30 am
Must try: Manhattan, Orange Granita Sparkle, Murgh ke Parche
Cost for Two: ₹ 1800 (approx.)


4. Rhythm And Blues, Nagpur

Rhythm and Blues is crowded, noisy and only semi-lit. The waiters are often MIA, and the place only sees humans (other than the bartender) after 10 pm. Sad scene? Nope! Just like every other pub in the world! One of the most iconic nightclubs in Nagpur, Rhythm and Blues will make you forget where you are; it could be Nagpur or Mumbai or Mombasa. The food is great, the drinks work wonders on your senses (even if they’re a bit pricey) and the crowds are friendly. What more could you ask for? FYI, they host karaoke nights on weekdays, and their New Year’s parties are known to be ‘killer’. Also read about Picnic spot in and around Nagpur
Location: Tuli International Hotel, Sadar
Timings: 11.00 am- 12.30 am
Must try: Frontier Mutton, all cocktails
Cost for Two: ₹ 1500 (approx.)


5. Ethyl, Nagpur

Trendy, upbeat and vivacious; Ethyl would be counted as the best pub in Nagpur for its food, music and ambiance. Overlooking the pool, this lounge-pub is a warm and welcoming space that promises a great time to friends, couples as well as colleagues. Luxurious seating, a painted ceiling, glass doors and walls that give it an ‘endless’ feel, live music and a well-stocked bar with an impressive wine list make Ethyl a great choice for an evening out in Nagpur.
Location: Radisson Blue, Wardha Road
Timings: 4.00 pm – 12.00 am
Must try: Blue Topaz, finger foods
Cost for Two: ₹ 3000 (approx.)
